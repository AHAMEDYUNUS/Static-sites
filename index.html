About Mustang

What is this?

The Ford Mustang is a beast that commands the road with raw power and an unstoppable presence. Its aggressive design and roaring engine exude strength, turning every drive into a thrilling ride. A true monster on wheels, the Mustang is ready to dominate the streets and leave a legacy of performance.

First gen

MUSTANG 1964

The 1964 Ford Mustang is an iconic American muscle car that was first introduced on 1964, It was built on the Ford Falcon platform, offering a stylish design and powerful engine options, including a 260-cubic-inch V8. The 1964 Mustang set the stage for the muscle car era and remains a beloved classic today.

Shelby Gens

Mustang Shelby Gt

The Shelby GT350, introduced in 1965, featured a 289-cubic-inch V8 engine and enhanced suspension for superior handling. These cars became legendary for their powerful performance and distinct styling, cementing their place in automotive history.
